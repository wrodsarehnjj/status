Cookie Clicker Multiplayer

What is CC‑MP?

CC‑MP is a community‑made project that brings multiplayer experiences to Cookie Clicker. Depending on the server, it can include rooms, shared progress, presence, and chat.

How it works (high level)

A client‑side mod connects to a CC‑MP server to exchange gameplay events (like clicks or session info) with other players in the same room. Exact features vary by server.

What CC‑MP is not

  • Not official or endorsed by DashNet/Orteil.
  • Not guaranteed to be available 24/7; servers may go up/down.
  • Not a paid product; avoid anyone selling “access”.
  • Not a promise of fairness on public servers—admins may enforce their own rules.

Notes

Always use trusted sources for mods. Keep backups of your Cookie Clicker save. Follow the rules of whichever server you join.
